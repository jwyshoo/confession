A poem for you

In quiet moments, I find you there, A thought, a wish, like whispered air. Your smile, a sun that warms my day, In your light, I’d gladly stay.

A heartbeat quickens, words unsaid, In your eyes, my thoughts are read. If only courage could match desire, To tell you that you light my fire.

But for now, I’ll dream and wait, Hoping fate brings us closer, mate. For in the silence, I find it true, My heart, it beats a little more for you.
